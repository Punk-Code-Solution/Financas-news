Our Mission

Finanças News was created to save time for people who need to stay informed about the global economy and crypto markets. In a world flooded with long-form noise, we democratize access to financial news that is fast, impartial and straight to the point.

Editorial Method

Every analysis published on Finanças News goes through a three-layer process:

  • Multi-source collection — RSS feeds from outlets such as G1 Economia, InfoMoney, Cointelegraph, Investing.com and Exame.
  • Data cross-check — Real-time quotes (AwesomeAPI), central-bank indicators (Selic, IPCA, USD) and the portal’s own archive.
  • AI editorial analysis — Original text with macro context, scenarios and practical guidance for readers.

We always keep the link to the original report and are transparent about the use of artificial intelligence in editorial production.

AI-Assisted Curation

We combine cutting-edge technology with journalism. An AI engine continuously scans the most reliable Brazilian and global financial sources.

The AI does not merely summarize stories — it produces original editorial analyses with market data, archive trends and the real impact on your wallet.
