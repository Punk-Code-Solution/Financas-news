This Policy explains how Finanças News, a portal maintained by Punk Code Solution, handles personal data when you use the site. Processing follows Brazil’s General Data Protection Law — LGPD (Law No. 13.709/2018) and other applicable rules.

1. Data we may process

  • Newsletter: email address provided voluntarily and subscription date;
  • Technical data: IP address, browser, device, pages visited, timestamps and security logs;
  • Local preferences: light/dark theme, language and reading preferences in the browser (localStorage / cookies);
  • Ads and metrics: identifiers, cookies and interaction data processed by enabled providers.

We do not ask for financial account data, identity documents or banking passwords to read the news.

2. Purposes and legal bases

Data may be used to:

  • deliver the newsletter and manage subscriptions;
  • keep preferences, performance and portal security;
  • prevent abuse, fraud and unauthorized access;
  • produce usage statistics and show ads when enabled;
  • comply with legal obligations and respond to rights requests.

Depending on the activity, processing may rely on consent, performance of a requested service, legitimate interest, legal obligation or regular exercise of rights.

3. Cookies and local storage

We use local storage and cookies to remember interface and language preferences. Third-party services such as Google AdSense, when enabled, may use cookies to deliver and measure ads.

4. Sharing and processors

Data may be processed by vendors required to operate the site (hosting, database, newsletter, security, advertising). We do not sell email lists or personal data.

5. External and affiliate links

Links to news outlets, sources and partners follow those third parties’ own policies. Please read their privacy notices before sharing information.

6. International transfers

Some vendors may process data outside Brazil, with safeguards compatible with the LGPD when applicable.

7. Retention and security

We keep data for as long as needed for the stated purposes and apply reasonable technical and organizational security measures.

8. Your rights

Under the LGPD, you may request, when applicable: confirmation and access; correction; anonymization, blocking, portability or deletion; information about sharing; and withdrawal of consent.

9. Children and adolescents

The portal does not intentionally collect children’s data. Guardians may request review and deletion.

10. Updates and contact

This Policy may be updated. To exercise rights or ask privacy questions, use the official channels of Punk Code Solution.
